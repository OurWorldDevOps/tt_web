A fun project by TheSudoersClub/Commumity aimed at simplifying the process of checking and maintaining a time table easier for both teachers and students.

About TimeLineUp

TimelineUp is a user-friendly mobile app designed to empower students with real-time access to their timetables. With its sleekand intuitive interface, students can easily view their schedules in a visually appealing timeline format.
